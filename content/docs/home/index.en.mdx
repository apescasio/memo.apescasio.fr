This is a simple way to keep track of my journey + evolution, and maybe share a bit with others.
## Search 

Click the "Search" icon, or press the keys "Ctrl + K" and then search for a keyword like "PowerShell" for example.

You can also switch "folders" by clicking on "Home" at the top left.
